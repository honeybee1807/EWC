We offer salvaged goods, including chicory and other recovered items, to reduce waste and provide affordable, sustainable options for our community.

Why Choose Salvaged Goods

Salvaged goods are affordable, eco‑friendly, and help reduce landfill waste. Choosing them supports sustainability and community empowerment.

Seasonal or Limited Stock

Some items are available seasonally or in limited quantities. Visit regularly to discover new finds and unique products.

How to Purchase

Customers can purchase salvaged goods directly at our facility. Payment options include cash and card. Contact us for bulk orders or special requests.

Community Impact

By offering salvaged goods, we create jobs, support local families, and promote recycling awareness in Estcourt.

Safety & Quality Assurance

All salvaged goods undergo safety and quality checks to ensure they meet community standards and are safe for reuse.

Interested in salvaged goods or chicory products? Reach out today to learn more or place an order.
