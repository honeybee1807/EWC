Our Story

Founded as a family business, Estcourt Waste Collectors grew from humble beginnings into a trusted recycling and salvaged goods provider serving KwaZulu-Natal.

Our Mission & Vision

We aim to transform waste into opportunity, building a cleaner Estcourt while empowering communities through recycling and sustainability.

Why Recycling Matters

Recycling reduces landfill waste, conserves resources, and creates jobs. It’s not just about waste — it’s about building a sustainable future.

Our Values

  • Sustainability
  • Community Empowerment
  • Integrity & Reliability
  • Innovation

Our Team Members

We are proud of our dedicated staff who make recycling possible every day.

Leadership Team

Our leadership team combines decades of experience in waste management, community development, and sustainable innovation.

Community Partnerships

We collaborate with schools, local businesses, and NGOs to promote recycling awareness and community empowerment.

Customer Stories

Families and businesses in Estcourt share how recycling and salvaged goods have made a difference in their lives.

Impact in Numbers

Over 10,000 tons recycled since 2010.

Awards & Recognition

We are proud members of the Institute of Waste Management of Southern Africa and recipients of local sustainability awards.

Commitment to Sustainability

We continuously improve our processes to minimize environmental impact and maximize community benefit.

Innovation in Recycling

From chicory by-products to salvaged goods, we innovate by finding new uses for materials that would otherwise go to waste.

Future Goals

Our vision is to expand recycling facilities, create more jobs, and lead the way in sustainable waste management in South Africa.

How You Can Help

Join us by recycling at home, supporting salvaged goods, and spreading awareness. Together, we can build a cleaner future.
