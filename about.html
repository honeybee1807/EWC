How It Started

A Family Initiative, A Community Movement

Estcourt Waste Collectors was born out of a simple belief: that a cleaner town is a stronger town. Started as a small, family-run operation in Estcourt, EWC has grown into one of the most trusted recycling services in the region.

From our early days with a single collection vehicle to a growing fleet serving hundreds of households and businesses, every step of our journey has been rooted in community empowerment and environmental responsibility.

Our Mission

Renewing resources unclocking opportunity.

EWC exists to give Estcourt a cleaner environment, a stronger circular economy, and a waste service that genuinely works — one household, one business, one bale at a time.
