Our Story

Started as a small family initiative in Estcourt, EWC has grown into a trusted recycling partner. From one truck in 2010 to serving hundreds of households and businesses today, our journey is rooted in community and sustainability.

Our Mission & Vision

We aim to transform waste into opportunity — building a cleaner Estcourt while empowering communities through recycling and sustainability.

Why Recycling Matters

Recycling reduces landfill waste, conserves resources, and creates jobs. For Estcourt, it means cleaner streets, healthier families, and new opportunities.
