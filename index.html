Who We Are

Estcourt Waste Collectors (EWC) is a family‑owned recycling company dedicated to keeping our town clean, reducing landfill waste, and empowering the community through sustainable practices. For over 25 years, we’ve provided reliable collection services, eco‑friendly recycling solutions, and community outreach programs that make a real difference.

Our mission is simple: to transform waste into opportunity, protect the environment, and build a greener future for Estcourt and beyond.
