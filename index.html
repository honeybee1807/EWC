Who We Are

Estcourt's Trusted Recycling Partner

Estcourt Waste Collectors (EWC) is a family-owned business dedicated to keeping our town clean, reducing landfill waste, and empowering the community through sustainable recycling practices.

For over 25 years we have provided reliable waste collection, eco-friendly recycling solutions, and community outreach that make a real difference across KwaZulu-Natal.
