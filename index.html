Our Recycling Facilities

About Us

We are a family-owned business serving Estcourt with recycling, salvaged goods, and chicory production. Our mission is sustainability, service excellence, and community empowerment.

Our Services

  • Household & Business Waste Collection
  • Recycling Depot (paper, plastics, metals, glass, e-waste)
  • Buy-Back Centre

Salvaged Goods & Chicory

We recover and resell salvaged goods such as furniture, appliances, and building materials. We also process chicory and agricultural by-products to support local sustainability.

Community & Sustainability
